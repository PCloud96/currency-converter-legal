Privacy Policy

Currency Converter by Cloud Motion Lab LLC
Last updated: April 12, 2026

Information We Collect

Currency Converter collects minimal anonymous analytics data through TelemetryDeck to improve the app experience. This data does not include any personally identifiable information. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

Analytics

We use TelemetryDeck, a privacy-first analytics service, to collect anonymous usage signals such as which features are used and general app performance metrics. TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, does not track users across apps, and is fully GDPR compliant. No personal data is transmitted.

Subscriptions and Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives anonymous identifiers from Apple to track purchase state. We do not have access to your payment information.

Exchange Rate Data

The app fetches live exchange rates from third-party APIs. These requests include the currency codes being converted but no personal information.

Data Storage

All user preferences, favorite currencies, and conversion history are stored locally on your device. We do not operate servers that store your data.

Third-Party Services

  • TelemetryDeck (anonymous analytics)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Apple StoreKit (in-app purchases)

Children's Privacy

Currency Converter does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and collects no personal data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
